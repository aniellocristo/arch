915 Spence St

Pontiac, MI

Mediums Used:

AutoCAD, Sketchup, Illustrator

Most work done for Habitat for Humanity was rehabilitation. Due to the high foreclosure inventory in Oakland County at the time it made the most sense. 915 Spence was the most memorable of several homes I assisted in remodeling. I was responsible for design proposal packages submitted to each city. This included, but was not limited to Site, Demolition, Architecture and Section Plans.
