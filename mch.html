MCH Property Management

Royal Oak, MI

Mediums Used:

AutoCAD, Sketchup, Illustator

MCH Property Management decided it was time for a move from Downtown Detroit to Royal Oak, MI. While moving, it was also decided the new location should be a contrast to the old traditionally designed office. Without a doubt, modern was a must. The location was a vacant space built in the 1970s... still with all original 70's materials and various fixtures. Design approach was to remove several sections within the exterior walls to let in more natural light. Maximizing the glass surface also complemented the metal exterior finishes.
