Henry Ford Cottage Medical Center

Grosse Pointe Woods, MI

Mediums Used:

AutoCAD, Sketchup, Illustrator

In October 2007 Henry Ford Health System acquired Cottage Hospital in Grosse Pointe MI. Just a few short miles from here are two other hospitals each owned and operated by two other systems. In such a competitive market, patient experience is everything. Henry Ford re-purposed this location into a medical center focused on holistic healing, and what better way to accomplish this by removing concrete and adding some landscape! We proposed a curved main walkway with two exit points, one to the main road and the other to the main entrance. Both adorned with a trellis.
